What Hearth does

Identity injection
Your preferences, communication style, values, and context get injected into every AI conversation. No more starting from scratch.
Constitutional constraints
Define what the AI should and shouldn't do. Hearth enforces your principles, not the platform's engagement metrics.
Memory that matters
Not just what you said, but who you are. Structured memory that shapes behavior, not just retrieves facts.

Install (Developer Preview)

1
Clone the repo
Download or clone from GitHub to your local machine.
2
Open Chrome Extensions
Navigate to chrome://extensions and enable Developer Mode (top right).
3
Load unpacked
Click "Load unpacked" and select the Hearth extension folder.
4
Configure your identity
Open the extension popup and complete the onboarding to set up your profile.
